Essential Roof Maintenance: A Homeowner's Complete Guide

Regular roof maintenance is essential for protecting your home and maximizing your roofing system's lifespan, especially in Calgary's diverse climate. Key maintenance tasks include biannual inspections to check for damaged shingles, loose nails, and water damage, along with regular gutter cleaning to prevent drainage issues. Homeowners should watch for warning signs like interior water stains, excessive granule loss, and higher energy bills. While some maintenance can be done independently, professional services offer comprehensive inspections, expert repairs, and warranty documentation. Regular maintenance proves cost-effective by extending roof life, reducing repairs, and maintaining home value.
