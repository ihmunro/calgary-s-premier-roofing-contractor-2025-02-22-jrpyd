About Premier Roofing

Founded in 2000, Premier Roofing began with a simple mission: to provide Calgary homeowners with the highest quality roofing services backed by integrity and professionalism. What started as a small family business has grown into one of Calgary's most respected roofing contractors, serving thousands of satisfied customers throughout the region.

Our core values of excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in using only the finest materials, employing skilled craftsmen, and maintaining transparent communication throughout every project. Our commitment to these principles has earned us numerous industry awards and, more importantly, the trust of our community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most residential roof installations are completed within 2-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. Larger projects or those requiring additional repairs may take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
We offer a comprehensive 25-year warranty on materials and workmanship. This includes coverage for leaks, material defects, and installation issues. Additionally, manufacturer warranties on materials typically range from 30-50 years.
Yes, we have extensive experience working with insurance companies and can assist you throughout the claims process. Our team will document damage, provide detailed estimates, and work directly with your insurance adjuster to ensure you receive fair coverage.
